Frequent questions

About the integrations.

What's the commission on OTA bookings?
Travelity charges 1.9% commission on bookings made through OTA integrations. Direct bookings (back-office or your widget) carry no commission. The OTA itself charges its own commission separately, which Travelity factors into per-channel pricing automatically.
How do pricing changes propagate to OTAs?
Real-time. When you save a pricing change, the update is pushed to every connected OTA in the same transaction. There are no batch jobs or daily reconciliations — what you see in the back-office is what's live on the OTAs within seconds.
What happens when a guest cancels via an OTA?
Cancellations from any channel land in the same orders system, with full audit trail. Refund flows are gateway-integrated and work the same way regardless of which channel the booking came from. Cancellation policies you've configured per product are enforced consistently.
Can I sell different products on different OTAs?
Yes. Per-option channel visibility lets you decide which products and options appear on which channels. You can list a tour on GetYourGuide and Klook but hide it from Viator, or list a VIP option only on your widget — all from one settings panel.

Operations

When things go sideways.

Incident response

We have an on-call team available 24/7 with documented procedures for every category of incident. If a breach involves your data, we'll notify you within 24 hours and follow up in writing within 72, in line with GDPR requirements.

Business continuity

We can restore your data to any second within the last 7 days. Daily snapshots are kept for 30 days. Standby copies sit ready in a separate region. If something catastrophic happens, our worst-case recovery target is 4 hours — and you'd lose at most 1 hour of activity.

Data residency

Your data is stored in the European Union (Frankfurt). Payment data is processed through Stripe's compliant infrastructure. Your data doesn't leave the EU unless you specifically connect a service that requires it — for example, certain OTA integrations route through the US, and we disclose every one of them.
