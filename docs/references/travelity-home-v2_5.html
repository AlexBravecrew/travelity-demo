All-in-one channel management

Channels & integrations, built in.

Travelity is the booking engine, the channel manager, and the live OTA integrations — all in one platform. Sell direct, embed a widget, or list on the marketplaces. One inventory, one source of truth.

  • Live channel management Real-time inventory and pricing sync across every channel you sell on.
  • Native OTA integrations Two-way connections with GetYourGuide, Viator, and Klook — no third-party connector required.
  • Embeddable widget Take direct bookings on your own site with three lines of code. Your branding, your guest, no commission.
  • One source of truth Bookings from every channel land in the same orders queue, with full attribution and audit trail.
Built for daily operations

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Six core capabilities, modeled the way real travel businesses actually work — not the way booking software thinks they should.

Real-time availability

Inventory locked the instant a booking starts. Across every channel, at the same time. No more double-bookings, ever.

Booking management

Every order — direct, widget, or OTA — in one queue. Edit, refund, reassign, generate documents. Full history, full audit trail.

Daily operations

Today's departures, who's driving, who's guiding, who's booked. The view your operations manager opens at 8am.

Custom proposals

Build a quote, send a signed link, get paid. Custom group inquiries, B2B partner orders, and multi-day packages — all closed online.

Customer database

Every booking creates a customer record. Repeat guests, contact history, total spend. Stop reconstructing relationships from WhatsApp threads.

Payments & refunds

Stripe-powered checkout, multi-currency support, refunds with audit trail. Bank-level encryption everywhere money moves.
